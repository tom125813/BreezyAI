How to Configure Breezy

Welcome to the Breezy configuration tutorial! This step-by-step guide will help you set up Breezy to automate your social media responses effectively. Learn how to link your social accounts, personalize AI interactions, and optimize your customer engagement seamlessly.

1. Link Your Social Accounts

Connect your social media platforms to manage all interactions from a centralized dashboard.

  • Connect Your X (Twitter) Account: Manage DMs and mentions seamlessly.
  • Connect Your Facebook Page: Automate responses to comments and messages.
  • Automate Instagram DMs: Ensure timely replies to your Instagram audience.
  • Enable WhatsApp Automation: Utilize our API for streamlined WhatsApp interactions.

3. Optimize Your Settings

Fine-tune Breezy's settings to align with your business goals and operational workflows.

  • Automate Response Scheduling: Set specific time frames or keep responses active 24/7.
  • Configure Security Protocols: Ensure your data is protected with advanced encryption and access controls.
  • Set Up In-Depth Analytics: Monitor performance and optimize strategies based on comprehensive insights.

4. Launch & Monitor

Activate your automated responses and keep track of their performance.

  • Activate Automation: Launch your automated responses across all connected platforms.
  • Monitor Performance: Use our analytics tools to track engagement and response effectiveness.
  • Optimize Strategies: Adjust settings based on analytics to continuously improve customer interactions.
