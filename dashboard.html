Frequently Asked Questions


How do I purchase credits?
Go to the Purchase Credits section, select a package or use the custom slider, and proceed to checkout.
How can I set up auto-responses during specific hours?
In the Configuration section, enable the Auto Responses checkbox and select your desired start and end times.
How do I customize my brandâ€™s tone?
Navigate to the Configuration section and choose from the available tone styles that best represent your brand's personality.
How do I set up human intervention for complex queries?
In the Configuration section, enable AI Detection for automatic identification of complex queries or manually add keywords for specific cases.
What analytics are available, and how do I interpret them?
The analytics dashboard provides insights into daily replies, credit usage, response effectiveness, and human intervention needs. Click "View More" to see detailed charts.
How do I get notified when credits are low?
In the Purchase Credits section, enable the Notify When Credits Are Low checkbox and provide your email.
How do I change my account settings?
Go to the Account Settings section, where you can update your password, email, and notification preferences.
