Help & FAQs

How do credits work?

Each automated reply costs 1 credit. Your account starts with free credits, and you can purchase additional bundles in the dashboard any time.

How many credits are used per message?

Typically, each AI-generated response uses 1 credit. If the AI responds multiple times in a single interaction, each response consumes an additional credit.

Can I customize the AI’s brand tone?

Yes! Our configuration panel offers 15 preset tones. You can choose one that aligns best with your brand’s personality and voice.

Is my data secure?

We use enterprise-grade encryption and maintain strict compliance with industry standards to keep your information safe.

What if I run out of credits?

The AI will pause responding once credits are depleted. You can buy more credits at any time from your dashboard, or you can continue replying manually.

Is there a monthly subscription fee?

No. We operate on a credit-based pay-as-you-go model. You only pay when you need more replies, making it more flexible and cost-effective.

How do I handle complex messages?

You can configure advanced FAQs and brand voice settings. The AI will interpret user messages contextually, ensuring appropriate and accurate replies.

Can I integrate with multiple social media accounts?

Absolutely. We offer multi-platform integrations so you can manage replies across all your channels from a single dashboard.

Does the AI learn over time?

Yes, our AI model refines its understanding through interactions, constantly improving response accuracy and relevance.

Need more help?

Contact our support team at support@autoreply.ai and we’ll be glad to assist!
